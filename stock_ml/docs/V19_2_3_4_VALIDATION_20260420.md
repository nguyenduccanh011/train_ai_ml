## Keep/Discard Decision
- `KEEP`:
  - `V19.1` as primary production baseline (best composite and highest model TotalPnL/PF).
  - `V19.2` as risk-controlled fallback variant when drawdown cap priority > total return.
- `DISCARD`:
  - `V19.3` and `V19.4` for production at current settings (no performance edge over V19.2, lower return).
